Sales Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing sales trends and KPIs across regions, products, and time periods.

Problem Statement

Sales teams struggled to identify underperforming regions and products due to scattered data sources.

Approach

  • Used Tableau to create an interactive dashboard with drill-down functionality.
  • Integrated sales data from SQL databases for real-time updates.

Results

The dashboard helped reduce analysis time by 50%, resulting in a 10% increase in regional sales.
