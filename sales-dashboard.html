Sales Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing sales trends and KPIs across regions, products, and time periods. This project showcases how data visualization can drive actionable insights and improve sales strategies.

Problem Statement

Sales teams struggled to identify underperforming regions and products due to scattered data sources and manual reporting processes.

Approach

  • Interactive Tableau dashboard: Drill-down functionality for detailed analysis.
  • Real-time data: Integrated SQL databases for accurate reporting.
  • Custom filters: Enabled analysis by product categories, regions, and time periods.

Results

The dashboard reduced analysis time by 50%, enabling sales managers to focus on underperforming areas. This led to a 10% increase in regional sales within the first quarter of implementation.
