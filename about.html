About Me

Hi, I'm Logeshwaran Inbaraj, but you can call me Loki! I’m a data enthusiast passionate about turning numbers into compelling stories. With over 6 years of experience in data analytics and business solutions, I’ve worked on impactful projects ranging from improving customer acquisition, supply chain and detecting fraud to streamlining workflows and enhancing decision-making processes.

I hold an MBA from Indian Institute of Management Raipur, where I built a strong foundation in business strategy and decision-making, followed by a Master’s in Computing - Data Science from Dublin City University, where I honed my technical expertise. My academic journey is complemented by a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, equipping me with a robust mix of technical and functional skills.

Whether it’s building interactive dashboards, developing machine learning models, or optimizing business processes, I thrive on uncovering insights hidden in the data and translating them into actionable strategies.

When I’m not delving into data, you’ll find me exploring the latest tech tools, catching a good movie, or discovering cool spots in Dublin.
